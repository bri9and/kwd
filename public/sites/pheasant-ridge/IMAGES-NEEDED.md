## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG for photos (compressed for web, ~200-400KB each), PNG for logo (transparent background)
- **Color tone**: Warm natural tones — greens, earth tones, golden light. Complement the hunter green (#1B4332) and burnt orange (#CC5500) palette.
- **Subjects**: Emphasize wooded fairways, rolling terrain, natural beauty, wildlife habitat feel. No people required but optional.
- **Season**: Spring/summer preferred — full foliage, lush conditions.
- **Quality**: High resolution originals, will be displayed at various sizes via CSS object-cover.

## Color Palette Reference

- Hunter Green: `#1B4332`
- Burnt Orange: `#CC5500`
- Cream: `#FAF5EB`
- Forest accent: `#2D5016`
