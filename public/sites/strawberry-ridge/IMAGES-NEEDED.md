# Strawberry Ridge Golf Course — Images Needed

All images go in `/public/sites/strawberry-ridge/`.

## Required

| File | Purpose | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| `logo.png` | Course logo | Transparent PNG, ~150px wide for hero, ~60px for top bar |
| `hero.jpg` | Hero background | Wide landscape shot of the ridge-top setting, 1920x1080 min |
| `course1.jpg` | Gallery — fairway | Ridge-top fairway with rolling terrain |
| `course2.jpg` | Gallery — valley view | Scenic valley/countryside panorama from the course |
| `course3.jpg` | Gallery — green | Green with mature tree line backdrop |
| `course4.jpg` | Gallery — terrain | Rolling hills, elevation changes |
| `bar.jpg` | Bar & restaurant section | Clubhouse bar interior or patio with food/drinks |

## Specs

- **Hero**: 1920x1080 minimum, landscape orientation, good for dark overlay text
- **Gallery**: 1200x675 minimum (16:9 ratio preferred), will display at ~600x288 in 2x2 grid
- **Bar**: 1920x800 minimum, landscape, good for dark overlay text
- **Logo**: Transparent PNG, crisp at both 60px and 150px widths
