## Photography Notes

- Emphasize the **historic character** — century-old trees, stone walls, classic architecture
- Beaver River valley panoramas are the hero shots
- Avoid modern/commercial look — aim for timeless, traditional golf photography
- Morning or golden hour lighting preferred for warmth
- The course was established in 1898 — any archival/vintage imagery would be a bonus
