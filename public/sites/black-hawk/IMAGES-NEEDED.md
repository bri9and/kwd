# Black Hawk Golf Course — Images Needed

All images go in `/public/sites/black-hawk/`.

## Required Files

| File | Description | Recommended Size |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| `logo.png` | Black Hawk Golf Course logo (transparent PNG) | 300x180 or similar |
| `hero.jpg` | Aerial or wide-angle hero shot of the course — emphasize the scale of 36 holes | 1920x1080 |
| `course1.jpg` | Fairway view from Course 1 | 1200x675 |
| `course2.jpg` | Scenic green or hole view from Course 2 | 1200x675 |
| `course3.jpg` | Tree-lined hole or signature landscape shot | 1200x675 |
| `course4.jpg` | Panoramic or elevated course overview | 1200x675 |
| `taverne.jpg` | Hawk's Nest Taverne interior or exterior — bar/restaurant atmosphere | 1920x1080 |
| `banquet.jpg` | Events/tournament space — could be outing group photo, banquet setup, or tournament tee box | 1920x1080 |

## Notes

- Hero and section backgrounds (hero.jpg, taverne.jpg, banquet.jpg) are used as full-width background images with dark overlays — they should be high-resolution and work well when darkened.
- Gallery images (course1-4.jpg) display at roughly 600x288 on desktop — optimize for web but keep crisp at 2x.
- Logo should be transparent PNG with no background — works on both dark and light backgrounds.
- All images should represent the actual Black Hawk Golf Course property at 644 Blackhawk Rd, Beaver Falls, PA 15010.
