## Style Notes

- **Color palette**: Dark slate (#1E293B), gold (#D4AF37), black (#0D0D0D), cream (#FAF5EB)
- **Mood**: Shadow and lake mystique — dark water reflections, long afternoon shadows, golden hour light
- **Aspect ratios**: Hero 16:9 minimum width 1920px; gallery images 16:9 at 1200x675px minimum; restaurant 16:9
- **Logo**: Works best on both dark (#1E293B) and light (#FAF5EB) backgrounds — consider white/gold version with transparency
